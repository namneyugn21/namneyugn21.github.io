Realtorest

Realtorest is a web-application designed for realtors to showcase their properties and to connect with potential clients. The website is focused on minimalistic design that can help users navigate the site with ease. Realtorest also incorporates Google Maps API for locating property.

Timeline: 4 months
Framework & Software: Spring Boot and Thymeleaf
Languages: Java, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PostgreSQL
Role: UX/UI Design, Visual + Interaction Design and Testing
